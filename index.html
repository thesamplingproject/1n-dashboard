Qualitative Themes

  • Packaging is consistently praised — child lock, metal tin and premium feel noted positively across multiple unprompted responses
  • Nicotine strength is the most common criticism — existing pouch and cigarette smokers want a higher mg option
  • Flavour and mint experience are strong positives; a minority note unwanted bitterness or an unusual dissolve sensation
  • Ingredient transparency is trusted and appreciated, particularly when communicated directly at point of sampling
  • Tin rattle flagged independently by multiple respondents — a consistent, actionable packaging point
